San Francisco Perl Mongers

Welcome to the once and future home of the San Francisco Perl Mongers!

We are a community of technology enthusiasts who hold regular meetings in San Francisco to discuss and learn about the Perl Programming Language.

This web site is undergoing a long-overdue redesign, which may take a while. In the meantime, please visit our Meetup page for meeting schedules and announcements:

http://www.meetup.com/San-Francisco-Perl-Mongers/

Join The Mailing List

The San Francisco Perl Mongers mailing list is open to Perl questions of all sorts and all levels. To subscribe send e-mail to:
sanfrancisco-pm-request@pm.org
...with a message body that says:
subscribe
You may also subscribe on the web, or view the list archives, at:
http://mail.pm.org/mailman/listinfo/sanfrancisco-pm

Thanks for visiting!
